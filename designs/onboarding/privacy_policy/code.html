Privacy Policy

Phobik (PBK) – Privacy Policy

Effective: January 24, 2026

1. Information We Collect

account_circle A. Account Information

We collect your name, email address, and age when you create a PHOBIK profile to personalize your mental wellness journey.

edit_note B. User Content

This includes mood logs, journals, and reflections you record within the app. This data is private and encrypted.

vital_signs C. Health & Biometric Data

To provide advanced mental health insights, PHOBIK analyzes facial micro-expressions and Heart Rate Variability (HRV). Processed locally on-device; raw images are never uploaded. Only derived numerical scores are stored with end-to-end encryption.

smartphone D. Device Information

We collect IP addresses, device types, and OS versions to ensure app compatibility and security.

groups E. Community Data

Interaction data within PHOBIK forums or peer support groups is stored to facilitate conversation and safety monitoring.

2. Community Features & Anonymity

PHOBIK is built on a foundation of safety. Community features use default anonymity. Your real name is never displayed to other users unless you explicitly choose to reveal it.

3. How We Use Information

Data is used to generate your Anxiety Score, provide personalized coping strategies, improve our AI algorithms, and facilitate peer connections.

4. How We Share Information

We do not, and will never, sell your personal or health data to third-party advertisers or data brokers.

We may only share data with service providers (e.g., cloud storage) necessary for app operation, or when required by law for safety reasons.

5. Your Rights & Choices

You have the right to access, correct, or delete your data at any time through the app settings. You may also request a copy of your data in a portable format.

6. Data Retention

We retain your information as long as your account is active. If you delete your account, your data is scrubbed from our production databases within 30 days.

7. Data Security

We use AES-256 encryption for data at rest and TLS 1.3 for data in transit. Regular security audits are conducted to maintain HIPAA-level compliance.

8. Children’s Privacy

PHOBIK is intended for individuals 18 years of age or older. We do not knowingly collect information from children. If we discover such data, we delete it immediately.

9. International Users

Your data may be transferred to and processed in the United States. We use Standard Contractual Clauses (SCCs) for cross-border data protection.

10. Changes to Policy

We may update this policy periodically. We will notify you of any significant changes via in-app notification or email.
